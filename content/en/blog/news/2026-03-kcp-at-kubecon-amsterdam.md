[KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-europe/) is just a **few days away**! The major European cloud native conference kicks off on Monday, March 23rd in Amsterdam, Netherlands, and the kcp project will be there with exciting content and activities on every day!

From talks and lightning sessions to a hands-on Contribfest, there are plenty of opportunities to learn about kcp and connect with our maintainers. You can meet us at the **Project Pavilion** on **Wednesday, March 25, from 14:00 to 17:00 CET at kiosk 18B** — feel free to drop by to chat about kcp or just say hi. 👋

Here's a full overview of all kcp activities at this year's KubeCon:
